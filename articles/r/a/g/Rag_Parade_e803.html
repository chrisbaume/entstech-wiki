Rag Parade

From EntsTech

RAG (Raising and Giving) Parade is the day when York Uni descends on town to raise money for charity. This usualy consists of fancy dress parade with a theme from each college, and a center stage on Parliament Square in town.

[edit] Previous Rag Parades

[edit] Rag Parade 08

The rig stared in town at 10am prompt, as we had to meet the council's electrician (who doesn't wait for people turning up late apparently!). There is a 16A supply in the public toilet building, which he wired us into. Power was linked out of the 16A in the back of FOH rack.

  • We took 4 blocks of ally deck. The ground is very uneven, so many chock blocks were needed to minimize the rake on the stage.

(A good technique for this kind of ground is to not tighten the stage legs until the stage is the right way up, assembled. That way play in the legs allows the stage to be set flat with minimal chocs. -Tom)

  • Two Powerdrive stands and 5m of truss were taken. We draped off the back of the stage and hung the RAG banner off it.
  • For PA we took the C-Rig PA (amps 2) in bi-amped mode, 4 mics and stands, FOH rack and a CD player.

URY ran the show, and we came back at the end to de-rig. Council's Electrician is uber-anal about the PATesting of equipment, and checked EVERY item of URY's in 2007, and wouldn't turn the power on until he had seen that everything had passed and had a sticker.
