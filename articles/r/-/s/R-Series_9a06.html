R-Series

[edit] R-Series Components

[edit] RT 112 F

Horn-loaded mid/high unit with integral passive crossover for demanding mid-to-long-throw applications. Designed specifically for use with RL 118 or RL 118 F subwoofers. Flexible linear-array design with 25° x 50° horn (convertible to 50° x 25°) generates powerful acoustical coupling with precise and accurate directivity. Aeroquip 8-position fly tracks for use with dedicated R-SERIES® rigging hardware are included.


Technical Data

  • Nominal power-handling capacity: 300 W RMS, 600 W prog.
  • Frequency response ±3 dB: 130 Hz – 18 kHz
  • Sound Pressure Level, 1W@1m*: 112 dB
  • Maximum SPL @1m*: 140 dB @ 10% THD
  • Nominale Impedance: 8 ohms
  • Low/mid woofer: 12"
  • High-frequency driver: 2"
  • Horn: 50° x 25° / 25° x 50°
  • Crossover frequency: 900 Hz, 12 dB/octave
  • Connections: 1x EP 4 m, 1x EP 4 f, 1x XLR m, 1x XLR f
  • Cabinet construction: 19 mm (3/4"), 13-ply birch plywood
  • Cabinet finish: 2-part polyurethane
  • Protective grille: Rugged grille cloth, black
  • Rigging hardware: 8x Aeroquip fly tracks
  • Weight: 75 kg (165 lb)
  • Dimensions (w x h x d): 60 x 65 x 86 cm (incl. wheels), 23-5/8" x 25-5/8" x 33-7/8"
  • Wheels: 4x 100-mm blue wheels

[edit] RL 118

18" horn-loaded subwoofer that delivers high sound pressure levels with a vast amount of headroom and minimal power compression. Intended for use in combination with HK AUDIO® RT 112 F or HK AUDIO® T-SERIES® mid/high units.


Technical Data

  • Nominal power-handling capacity: 600 W RMS, 1200 W prog.
  • Frequency response ±3 dB: 40 Hz – 250 Hz
  • Sound Pressure Level, 1W@1m*: 103 dB
  • Maximum SPL @ 1m*: 138 dB @ 10% THD
  • Nominal impedance: 8 ohms
  • Driver: 1x 18", 5.5" voice coil, Horn-loaded bandpass
  • Recommended crossover frequency: 80 - 150 Hz, 24 dB/oct.
  • Connections: 1x EP4 m, 1x EP4 f, 2x Speakon® NL 4
  • Cabinet construction: 19 mm (3/4"), 13-ply birch plywood
  • Cabinet finish: 2-part polyurethane
  • Protective grille: Rugged grille cloth, black
  • Weight: 64 kg (140.8 lb)
  • Dimensions (w x h x d): 60 x 65 x 86 cm***, 23-5/8" x 25-5/8" x 33-7/8"
  • Wheels: 4x 100 mm blue wheels
