Battle of the Bands

From EntsTech

Battle of the Bands (BOB) is one of the largest events in the EntsTech calender. The competition takes the form of a week of heats, followed by two semi-finals and the final.

[edit] BOB '08

Heats took place Wk4 Spring term, with semis Mon/Tues Wk6 and the final on Sat Wk6. The heats and semis were held in Goodricke, with the final in Vanbrugh.

See the Gallery for more pictures.

[edit] Heats

BOB Heats 2008

The heats ran from Monday the 28th Jan till Friday 1st Feb and were held in Goodricke Dining Hall. Stage went in about 14:00. Setup began at 16:00 meeting at Vanbrugh cupboard and driving the equipment to Goodricke due to the Vanbrugh-Goodricke bridge closure. Everything went smoothly and the re-wired dimmer rack worked perfectly. The get out was complete by 01:00 Saturday morning and we all popped down to the Lowther for last orders, met up with the YSTV / URY crews and ended up drinking dark lager home brew at Tom Barker's house till early in the morning.

[edit] Stage

10 blocks of stage were erected at the power cupboard end in a 5*2 formation. We could have probably got away with 8 blocks for the heats.

[edit] PA

A full A-Rig Band setup was used, Full Stack R-Series, LE Imitation Monitors, HK Premium Series Turbo Drumfill, Soundcraft Desk. Nothing was hired in.

[edit] Lighting

The lighting setup for the heats was kept to a bare minimum - 7m bar of truss along the back, and two can bars on Powerdrive stands at the front.

[edit] Semis

BOB semis 2008

The semis were held on Monday the 11th Feb and Tuesday the 12th in Goodricke Dining Hall. We stepped up the rig for the semis:

[edit] Stage

Exactly the same size as the Heats, 5*2. This is the largest stage you can get in the venue!

[edit] PA

A full A-Rig Band PA was supplied. We booked an Yamaha LS9 from JSS to run FOH however it was out on a gig so we ended up with an M7 instead! URY took their audio feed directly from the stage box on the second tailset.

[edit] Lighting

The back box of truss was draped over the top. Drapes were placed all around the back and sides of the stage. The whole rig was raised to the roof in Goodricke on 4 Doughty Strata Stands. Can Bars were placed on the front of the u-shaped truss, mini-mac's and strobe across the center section. Three blinders along the back bar. Robe and Abstact scans / revs were placed on the sides and in gaps along the middle and back.

[edit] Final

The final was held on Saturday 16th Feb. The get-in took two days, starting at 7.30pm after the dinner service on the Friday, until 5.15am the next morning, and then 12pm until the gig opened at 9pm. The rig was absolutely massive and took loads of effort. The get-out started as soon as the gig finished at 2am, and was completed by 7.45am.

[edit] Power

Careful planning of the power budget is needed to ensure it doesn't trip during the gig. Remember to pull maximum current off each of the 3 phases, as it is essential to balance the load across them all.
