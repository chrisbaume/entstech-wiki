[edit] Flightcase races

A series of impromptu races in Vanbrugh Dining Hall saw a certain crew member slam into a wall plug, sending it flying into the wall. The plug in question has since been repaired, but as the wall was so damaged, the plug is mounted on a large panel of plywood. Reconstruction of the event which never happened

[edit] Get out of Jail Free Cards

  • The SPX 990 has a patch labelled 96 dB limiter in it.... it don't do 'owt but it has kept the enjoyment police off our backs in the past.
  • A piece of electrical tape over the mic on a sound level meter will reduce the reported level by approx 6 dB.

[edit] White Van Man

  • Did you know that is is entirely possible to hand break turn and powerslide a Mercedes Sprinter.
  • The normal highway code does not apply when driving a white van especially if it has a yellow go-faster stripe. That's the law that is.
  • It is possible to overload a long wheel base Sprinter by approximately 1200 kg. Just keep it below 40 and for gods sake take the corners slow.
  • The EntsTech vans have been crashed by everyone who has ever driven them. Somebody crashed into a tree, somebody into a parked car, someone got stuck under a bridge, and somebody even set fire to one.
  • If the rear doors locks are playing up just put a ratchet strap round the whole van.
  • On one trip with a particularly insecure load, the rear door locks failed and the doors started to open, threatening to spill the van's contents (including the power trunk) onto the bonnet of a car.
  • A shopping trolley can be towed in relative safety at up to 15 mph as long as the occupant is under 14 stone.
  • The pecking order of motorized vehicles is as follows. Bear this in mind when deciding how to treat other drivers.
    1. Articulated lorries (you will never win an argument with one, and even if you do, you will lose against its driver)
    2. Other white van men
    3. Motorbikes
    4. Taxi drivers
    5. Boyracers
    6. Hairdressers with ridiculous cars (such as pick-ups)
    7. Anything that is more than 20% rust (old volvos, old nissan sunnys....)
    8. Boyracers with especially terrible cars
    9. Repmobiles (new bmw, new audi, etc)
    10. Bus drivers

[edit] Lighting Jockey

One of the perks of controlling the lights during disco rigs is that drunk (and let's face it... stupid) girls come up to you and request songs.

Perhaps the best example of this happened in 2007 when a particularly attractive girl kept catching eyes with Chris during a Goodricke gig. Chris didn't think much of it, as it happens everywhere he goes. However, as the last song was being played, the girl wandered up to him and presented him with her phone, on which she had written 'You're the best DJ ever! xx'.

Legend has it one lucky employee once got invited back to a hotel after being mistaken for the DJ and is believed to have actually obliged! Lucky Man.
