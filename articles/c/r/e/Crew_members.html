[edit] New crew

In a mass recuitment drive, all corners of the campus were searched in order to find the new cream of the tech crop. Countless assessments were carried out including 'Fastest to stack the PA with one hand LX taped behind their back' and 'First to work out you have to plug the mixer into the amps to get sound through' (Dan...you should have known better). These are the fine individuals that were selected to represent the best of the best in York University Tech Companies.
